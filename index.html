Strawberry Custard

A creamy dessert made with milk, sugar, and fresh strawberries — sweet and refreshing.

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tbsp custard powder
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Fresh chopped strawberries
Mix custard powder with cold milk, boil the rest of the milk with sugar, combine, and chill. Top with strawberries before serving.

Shakshuka

A spicy Middle Eastern dish of poached eggs in a rich tomato and pepper sauce.

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 2-4 eggs
Sauté onion, garlic, and bell pepper in olive oil. Add tomatoes and spices; simmer. Crack eggs into the sauce and cook covered until eggs set.

Chicken Kebabs

Tender, marinated chicken grilled on skewers — juicy, smoky, and full of flavor.

  • 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt and pepper
Mix all ingredients and marinate chicken for 2+ hours. Skewer and grill until cooked through and lightly charred.

Salad

A fresh, colorful mix of veggies tossed with a light, tangy dressing — crisp and healthy.

  • 1 cup lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper
Combine all veggies in a bowl. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour dressing over salad and toss well.
